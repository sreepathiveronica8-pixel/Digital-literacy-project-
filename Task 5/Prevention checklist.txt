Stay Safe Online – 
Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts and change them regularly
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for email, banking, and social media accounts
Never share OTPs, passwords, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank
Always check the website URL before entering personal or financial information (look for https://)
Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages
Use only trusted apps and download them from official sources like Google Play Store or App Store
Regularly update your phone and computer software to stay protected from security threats
Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts on such networks
💳 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
Double-check the receiver’s UPI ID or phone number before making any payment
Never approve unknown payment requests or “collect requests” on UPI apps
